Goku VS Frieza

"Gohan, get out of here", said the saiyan named Goku. Frieza triumphantly looks at Goku, "you think that I would just let
your son leave? I'm the strongest in the universe!"

"Dont you dare talk about my son like that", Goku said. "And what will you do about it, hmmm?", replied
Frieza. "I'm gonna take you down, that's what", Goku shot angrily.

Goku begins to blast Frieza with a ki blast. One of Frieza's minions survives and begins to sneak up behind Goku as they fight.
"I will prevail!", says Frieza. His minion gets into position and attacks Goku.

Goku catches the minion's attack, and throws the minion aside as Frieza hits Goku in the face with a powerful punch.
"What?!", Frieza exclaimed as the attack did not hurt Goku. Goku proceeds to grab Frieza's fist and brings the fist beside him.

"Let go of me!", Frieza shouted while trying to pull his fist out from Goku's grasp. It wouldn't budge.
"Impossible! I'm the strongest in the universe!", Frieza exclaimed while continuing to pull.
Finally, Goku let go of Frieza's fist, and fires a large blast at Frieza, blowing Frieza to the ground.

Goku flew down to the ground in front of Frieza, giving Frieza a cold stare. "You won't get away
with this, I'll be back!", Frieza exclaimed as it got up and flew away. Goku wasn't going to let him get away.
